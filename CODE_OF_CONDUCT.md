# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to creating a respectful and inclusive environment.

---

## Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful.
- Be constructive.
- Welcome new contributors.
- Focus on collaboration.

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment
- Personal attacks
- Discrimination
- Offensive language

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove inappropriate contributions.

---

Thank you for helping make this project welcoming for everyone.
